About Us

PREAMBLE

It is a Non-Governmental Organization, registered with Cooperate Affairs Commission (CAC) as Library and Information Sensitization Forum of Nigeria (LISFN) and with Kaduna State Government as Library and Information Sensitization Vanguard of Nigeria (LISVN). It was founded in 2017 due to the poor utilization and accessibility of libraries and Information Centers, and the nonchalant attitude displayed by some citizens of Nigeria towards librarianship and Information Services. The organization was named after series of activities that took place within Zaria community, which was meant to sensitize the community towards the importance of libraries and Information services. Hence, the struggle for the formation of this Organization can be traced to a group of visionary and vibrant Students of the Department of Library and Information Science, Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, who wanted to rescue the state of poor utilization and accessibility of libraries and Information Centers.

In the effort of the organization to elevate and boost the standard of Librarianship and Information Services, it engaged in myriad of activities, such as Organizing Annual Inter School Academic Competition, Seminars, Conferences, Journal Publications, Paper Presentation, Book Chapters, Workshops, Spelling Bee for Primary and Junior Secondary Schools, Advocating for Open Access, Sensitization on Information Literacy and Reading culture, Sensitization on Community Contemporary Issues and Social Vices, Research and Consultancy Services, Responding to Community Information Needs, Annual LISFN Week, Advocating for the Adoption of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), Mentoring young scholars through online monthly lecture series, Providing Free Access for Educational Scholarship for Children and Women among others.Â

AIM AND OBJECTIVES

  • Providing access to Information on sustainable development goals.
  • Sensitize the community on the benefits for the adoption of Sustainable development goals (SDGs)
  • Fill in the gap between Information rich and Information poor
  • Responding to community information need
  • Marketing of library information resources and services and bring library closer to the users
  • Expose communities to the use of library and Information resources and services
  • Help improve the standard of librarianship and the status of the library profession in Nigeria and the world at large
  • Collect and publish information of benefits to the public and members of the association for the promotion of the association objectives
  • Execute all lawful things towards achieving our entire objectives.
  • Organizing Academic Conferences, Seminars, Journal Publications, Book chapters, Workshops and spelling bee for young learners

FUNCTIONS

  • Supporting the development of research skills.
  • Support for library development.
  • Creating library awareness to the public.
  • Facilitating learning research through access to information.
  • Providing a forum for membership networking.
  • Promoting library and Information Science profession in our communities.
  • Developing skills through participation in professional development activities.

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

The following are list of programs and activities executed over the years.

  • In 2017 LISFN Organizes Annual week which include: sanitation and hygiene day; Public Lecture day; Sensitization day; Bring back youth to library day; Public Awareness Campaign day; etc. which is in line with United Nation Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • In 2018, LISFN went for school advocacy on Information literacy and reading culture, across some selected schools in Kaduna State.
  • From 2018 to date LISFN engaged in awareness campaign on contemporary issues such as disease, insecurity, drug abuse, etc.
  • From 2018 to date LISFN organizes annual spelling be for junior and primary Schools.
  • As at 24th to 26th of March, 2021 LISFN organize her first Annual International Academic Conference with the theme; Library and Information Services and Global Trends

  • In 2020 during Corona Virus Lockdown, LISFN organized a free online seminar for librarians and Information scientist where scholars across the globe are invited to discuss extensively on the Fate of Librarianship and Information Science in the Current Pandemic.
  • In 2021 LISFN organizes Free Online Lessons for SSCE Students across Africa, with Specialist from different disciplines.
  • In 2021 LISFN went for an outreach to Schools on the Fight against Fake News, Misinformation, Disinformation, Rumor and Negative utterances within Kaduna State during its 2021 Annual Week.
  • In 2021 LISFN organize its 2021 Free Annual Online Seminar with the theme: New Normal in the 21st Century Library and Information Services.
  • From 2021 to date LISFN organizes Free Monthly Online Lectures as an avenue for training young scholars to be effective in public speaking, academic publication and research writing.
  • LISFN 2022 programs are Workshop on research methodology, School Spelling Bee and International Conference, Academic Journal Publication among others.
