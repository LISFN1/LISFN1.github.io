LISFN ANNUAL WORKSHOP ON RESEARCH METHODOLOGY, 2023 (blended)

  • posted by yusuf Ishaq
  • Monday 23rd of October, 2023 to Saturday 28th October, 2023.

Call for Participation

Library and Information Sensitization Forum, Nigeria (LISFN) hereby invite Researchers, Lecturers, Students, Organizations, Institutions and other interested participants to a Six (6) days workshop on research methodology.

Venue: Ahmadu Bello University School of Post Graduate Auditorium Hall. Participants can also participate via ZOOM

"Freedom is not just a word, it is a way of life. As Liberians, we must continually strive towards true liberation of our minds, our hearts, and our nation."

THEME

Global Best Practices in Conducting Data Analysis.

OBJECTIVES

At the end of the workshop, participants would be able to understands:

1. Global Best Practices in Conducting a Qualitative Data Analysis
2.Global Best Practices in Conducting a Quantitative Data Analysis
3. Global Best Practices in Conducting a Mixed Data Analysis
4. Global Best Practices in High impact Journal Publication
5. Global Best Practices in the Choice of a Research Philosophy/Paradigms
